About Us

Cascade Crusaders offers some of the best sceneries nature could offer, from wild rapid waterfalls, beautiful mountain tops ,and calm riverbanks to majestic isles and coves. If you are looking to spend time with your friends, family and or loved ones, this is the place to do it. Not only do we offer fun water water activities, we also prioritize spending uninterrupted quality time with those you love. Offering other activities that can be enjoyed without being splashed by water such as hiking and sight seeing.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate friends who share a love of water activities, our company has safely navigated wild rivers for over a decade and a half. We believe in providing unforgettable experiences for family, friends, and loved ones, while protecting our natural water resources.
